LenMus Phonascus Basics

Phonascus, from latin 'the teacher of music', is a music education software that you can use to practice your music reading skills, improve your ear training abilities, or just learn the fundamental principles of music theory and language.

Modes of operation

LenMus Phonascus has two modes of operation:

  1. As a reader of interactive music books (eBooks); and
  2. As a tool to create and edit music scores and to play them back. This second functionality (score editor / play back) is now under development and is disabled in current version.

Reading eBooks: interactive books on music

Phonascus is designed as a fully interactive textbook. All exercises are fully integrated into the text. Music scores are not just images or pictures but fully interactive operational music scores that you can hear, in whole or just the measures you select. This enables you to put concepts into practice with immediate feeback and makes the subject matter both more accessible and more rewarding to you, as you can hear, test and put in practise inmediately any new concept introduced.

When the     button is pressed or when you select the 'Open books' command in the 'File' main menu, the eBook manager window is visible. At program start, this button is pressed and the eBook manager window is open. Explore its content and enjoy! Do not forget exploring the customizable exercises on eBook 'Single exercises'. See page eBook Manager for a description of the eBook manager window and its operation.
