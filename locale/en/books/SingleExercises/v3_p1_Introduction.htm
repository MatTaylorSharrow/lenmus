Single exercises

Appart of exercises included in the eBooks, the following general exercises (customizable to suit your needs at any moment) are available:

Exercises for ear training

  • Identify intervals
    The program will play ramdomly selected intervals and you must identify the interval played. You can choose the intervals you would like to practise.
  • Compare intervals
    The program will play two intervals, one after the other, and you must identify wich one is grater.

Exercises to practise theory

  • Intervals
    The program will show an interval on a score and you must identify it. You can choose the intervals you would like to practise.
  • Identify scales
    The program will show an scale on a score and you must identify it.
  • Identify key signatures
    Identify the key signature of a music score.

Music reading

  • Clefs reading
    Simple scores, just with notes, to train in reading a clef.
