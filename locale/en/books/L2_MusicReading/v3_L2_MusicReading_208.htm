4. Compound time of 12/8. 8th note tied to 16th notes

The compound time of 12/8 has four parts and each beat is occupied by a dotted quarter note. The accent is on the first one. Ii is a time similar to the 6/8 and 9/8 times but with four beats. The note occupying all the measure is the dotted whole note.
