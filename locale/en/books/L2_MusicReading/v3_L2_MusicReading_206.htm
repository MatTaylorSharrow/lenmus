2. Compound time of 9/8. Dotted quarter note

In this lesson the compound time of 9/8 is introduced, as well as two new rhythmic patterns. The 9/8 time has three beats and each beat is occupied by a 8th note. The measure is occupied by a dotted quarter note.

Exercises

Exercise 1 - Train your ear and practise the introduced rhythmic patterns.

Exercise 2 - Read scores containing the rhythms studied in this lesson.
